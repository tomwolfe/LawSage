## 5. YOUR RIGHTS AND OBLIGATIONS

### YOUR OBLIGATION
You are required to produce all documents and items in your possession, custody, or control that are described in this subpoena.

### YOUR RIGHTS
a) **Objection:** You may serve written objections to this subpoena within [Number] days of service. Objections must state the basis for the objection.

b) **Motion to Quash:** You may file a motion to quash or modify this subpoena if it:
   - Fails to allow reasonable time to comply
   - Requires disclosure of privileged or protected information
   - Subjects you to undue burden or expense
   - Requires disclosure of trade secrets or confidential information

c) **Compensation:** In certain circumstances, you may be entitled to compensation for the cost of producing documents.

### PROTECTION OF PRIVACY
If any of the requested materials contain personal or confidential information, you may:
a) Redact sensitive information (with notice)
b) File a motion for protective order
c) Produce materials under seal
