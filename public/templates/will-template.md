# LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT

OF

[FULL NAME]

I, [Full Name], residing at [Address], being of sound mind and memory, do hereby make, publish, and declare this to be my Last Will and Testament, hereby revoking all Wills and Codicils previously made by me.

## ARTICLE I - DECLARATIONS

### 1.1 Identity
I am [Full Name], sometimes referred to herein as "Testator."

### 1.2 Marital Status
☐ I am unmarried
☐ I am married to [Spouse's Full Name]
☐ I am divorced
☐ I am widowed

### 1.3 Children
I have [Number] children:
- [Child 1 Full Name], born [Date of Birth]
- [Child 2 Full Name], born [Date of Birth]
- [Child 3 Full Name], born [Date of Birth]

☐ I have no children

## ARTICLE II - APPOINTMENT OF EXECUTOR

### 2.1 Primary Executor
I appoint [Executor Full Name], residing at [Address], as the Executor of this Will.

### 2.2 Successor Executor
If the primary Executor is unable or unwilling to serve, I appoint [Successor Executor Full Name], residing at [Address], as successor Executor.

### 2.3 Powers of Executor
My Executor shall have full power and authority to:
- Pay my just debts, funeral expenses, and expenses of administration
- Sell, lease, or mortgage any property in my estate
- Distribute assets as directed herein
- Hire attorneys, accountants, and other professionals
- Make tax elections on behalf of my estate

## ARTICLE III - DISPOSITION OF PROPERTY

### 3.1 Specific Bequests
I give, devise, and bequeath the following specific gifts:

a) To [Beneficiary Name]: [Description of Property/Asset]

b) To [Beneficiary Name]: [Description of Property/Asset]

c) To [Beneficiary Name]: [Description of Property/Asset]

### 3.2 Residuary Estate
I give, devise, and bequeath all the rest, residue, and remainder of my estate, both real and personal, as follows:

☐ **Option A - To Spouse:**
To my spouse, [Spouse's Name], if they survive me by thirty (30) days.

☐ **Option B - To Children Equally:**
To my children, in equal shares, per stirpes.

☐ **Option C - Percentage Distribution:**
- [Beneficiary Name]: [Percentage]%
- [Beneficiary Name]: [Percentage]%
- [Beneficiary Name]: [Percentage]%

☐ **Option D - To Charity:**
To [Charitable Organization], for its general purposes.

### 3.3 Contingent Beneficiaries
If any beneficiary named herein does not survive me by thirty (30) days, their share shall pass to [Contingent Beneficiary Name].

## ARTICLE IV - GUARDIAN FOR MINOR CHILDREN

If I am survived by minor children, I appoint [Guardian Full Name], residing at [Address], as guardian of the person and property of my minor children.

If this guardian is unable or unwilling to serve, I appoint [Successor Guardian Full Name], residing at [Address], as successor guardian.

## ARTICLE V - TRUST PROVISIONS FOR MINOR BENEFICIARIES

### 5.1 Creation of Trust
If any beneficiary is under the age of [Age, typically 18 or 21] at the time of distribution, their share shall be held in trust by [Trustee Full Name] as Trustee.

### 5.2 Trustee Powers
The Trustee may use trust assets for the beneficiary's:
- Health, education, maintenance, and support
- Any other purpose the Trustee deems appropriate

### 5.3 Distribution Age
The remaining trust assets shall be distributed to the beneficiary when they reach age [Age].

## ARTICLE VI - GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 6.1 Simultaneous Death
If any beneficiary dies within thirty (30) days of my death, they shall be deemed to have predeceased me.

### 6.2 No Contest Clause
If any beneficiary contests this Will or any of its provisions, their share shall be revoked and distributed as if they had predeceased me.

### 6.3 Severability
If any provision of this Will is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

### 6.4 Governing Law
This Will shall be governed by the laws of the State of [State].
