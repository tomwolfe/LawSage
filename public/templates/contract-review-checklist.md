# CONTRACT REVIEW CHECKLIST

## PRELIMINARY REVIEW
- [ ] Is the contract properly titled?
- [ ] Are all parties clearly identified with legal names and addresses?
- [ ] Is the effective date clearly stated?
- [ ] Does the contract have a clear purpose/subject matter?

## ESSENTIAL TERMS
- [ ] Offer and acceptance clearly defined
- [ ] Consideration (what each party gives/receives) specified
- [ ] Terms are definite and certain
- [ ] Performance obligations clearly outlined
- [ ] Payment terms specified (amount, timing, method)
- [ ] Timeframes for performance established

## RISK ALLOCATION
- [ ] Indemnification clauses reviewed
- [ ] Limitation of liability provisions analyzed
- [ ] Insurance requirements specified
- [ ] Force majeure clause present and appropriate
- [ ] Termination clauses clear and fair

## LEGAL PROVISIONS
- [ ] Governing law specified
- [ ] Dispute resolution mechanism (arbitration/mediation/court)
- [ ] Severability clause included
- [ ] Entire agreement/integration clause present
- [ ] Amendment procedures outlined
- [ ] Assignment restrictions noted

## SIGNATURE BLOCKS
- [ ] Proper signature blocks for all parties
- [ ] Witnesses/notarization requirements met
- [ ] Execution date filled in

## ADDITIONAL CONSIDERATIONS
- [ ] Compliance with applicable regulations checked
- [ ] Tax implications considered
- [ ] Intellectual property rights addressed
- [ ] Confidentiality provisions adequate
- [ ] Non-compete/non-solicitation clauses appropriate (if applicable)
