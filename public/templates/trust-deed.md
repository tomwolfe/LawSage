# REVOCABLE LIVING TRUST DEED

This Revocable Living Trust Deed ("Trust Deed") is executed on [Date] by:

**GRANTOR/TRUSTOR:** [Full Name], residing at [Address] ("Grantor")

**TRUSTEE:** [Full Name], residing at [Address] ("Trustee")

**BENEFICIARY:** [Full Name], residing at [Address] ("Beneficiary")

(Collectively referred to as the "Parties")

## ARTICLE I - TRUST CREATION

### 1.1 Name of Trust
The Grantor hereby creates a revocable living trust known as the "[Full Name] Revocable Living Trust" (the "Trust").

### 1.2 Effective Date
This Trust shall be effective as of [Date] (the "Effective Date").

### 1.3 Revocability
This Trust is revocable by the Grantor during their lifetime. The Grantor reserves the right to amend, modify, or revoke this Trust in whole or in part at any time.

## ARTICLE II - TRUST PROPERTY

### 2.1 Initial Funding
The Grantor hereby transfers to the Trustee the property described in Schedule A attached hereto (the "Trust Property").

### 2.2 Additional Contributions
The Grantor or any other person may add property to the Trust at any time.

### 2.3 Acceptance by Trustee
The Trustee accepts the Trust Property and agrees to hold, manage, and distribute it according to the terms of this Trust.

## ARTICLE III - TRUSTEE POWERS AND DUTIES

### 3.1 Initial Trustee
The Grantor shall serve as the initial Trustee of this Trust.

### 3.2 Successor Trustee
Upon the death, incapacity, resignation, or removal of the initial Trustee, [Successor Trustee Full Name] shall serve as Successor Trustee.

### 3.3 Trustee Powers
The Trustee shall have the following powers:
- To invest and reinvest Trust assets
- To buy, sell, lease, or mortgage property
- To collect income and pay expenses
- To distribute income and principal to beneficiaries
- To hire attorneys, accountants, and investment advisors
- To file tax returns and make tax elections
- To borrow money and pledge Trust assets as security
- To maintain insurance on Trust property

### 3.4 Trustee Duties
The Trustee shall:
- Act in the best interests of the beneficiaries
- Keep accurate records of all Trust transactions
- Provide accountings to beneficiaries as required by law
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Exercise reasonable care and prudence in managing Trust assets

## ARTICLE IV - BENEFICIARY RIGHTS

### 4.1 Primary Beneficiary
During the Grantor's lifetime, the Grantor shall be the sole beneficiary of this Trust.

### 4.2 Distribution Standards
The Trustee shall distribute income and principal to the Grantor as the Grantor may request from time to time.

### 4.3 Remainder Beneficiaries

☐ **Option B - Percentage Distribution:**
- [Beneficiary Name]: [Percentage]%
- [Beneficiary Name]: [Percentage]%
- [Beneficiary Name]: [Percentage]%

☐ **Option C - Continuing Trust:**
The Trust shall continue for the benefit of [Beneficiary Name] until they reach age [Age].

## ARTICLE V - TRUST ADMINISTRATION UPON GRANTOR'S DEATH

### 5.1 Death of Grantor
Upon the Grantor's death, this Trust shall become irrevocable.

### 5.2 Payment of Debts and Expenses
The Successor Trustee shall pay:
- Funeral and burial expenses
- Expenses of last illness
- Administrative expenses of the Trust
- Valid creditors' claims
- Estate and inheritance taxes

### 5.3 Distribution of Remaining Assets
After payment of all debts and expenses, the remaining Trust assets shall be distributed to the remainder beneficiaries as specified in Section 4.3.

## ARTICLE VI - INCAPACITY PROVISIONS

### 6.1 Determination of Incapacity
The Grantor shall be deemed incapacitated upon written certification by [Number] licensed physician(s).

### 6.2 Successor Trustee Authority
Upon the Grantor's incapacity, the Successor Trustee shall manage the Trust assets for the benefit of the Grantor.

### 6.3 Recovery
If the Grantor recovers from incapacity, as certified by [Number] licensed physician(s), the Grantor shall resume serving as Trustee.

## ARTICLE VII - SPENDTHRIFT PROVISION

### 7.1 Restriction on Transfer
No beneficiary shall have the power to assign, encumber, or otherwise transfer their interest in the Trust, and such interest shall be protected from creditors' claims to the fullest extent permitted by law.

## ARTICLE VIII - GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 8.1 Governing Law
This Trust shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of [State].

### 8.2 Severability
If any provision of this Trust is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

### 8.3 Gender and Number
Words used in the singular include the plural, and words used in any gender include all genders.

### 8.4 Headings
Headings are for convenience only and shall not affect the interpretation of this Trust.
