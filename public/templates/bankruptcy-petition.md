## IMPORTANT NOTICES

### AUTOMATIC STAY
Upon filing this petition, an automatic stay goes into effect under 11 U.S.C. § 362. This stay prohibits most creditors from taking collection actions against you or your property.

### MEETING OF CREDITORS
You must attend a meeting of creditors (341 meeting) scheduled by the bankruptcy trustee. You will receive notice of the date, time, and location.

### FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT COURSE
Before receiving a discharge, you must complete an approved instructional course concerning personal financial management and file a certification of completion (Official Form 423).

### DISCHARGE
If you are an individual filing under Chapter 7, you will typically receive a discharge approximately 60-90 days after the meeting of creditors, assuming no objections are filed.
