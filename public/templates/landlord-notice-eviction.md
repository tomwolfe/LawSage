## IMPORTANT LEGAL DISCLAIMERS

**NOTICE TO LANDLORD:**

1. **Local Laws Vary:** Eviction procedures and notice requirements vary significantly by state, county, and city. This template may not comply with all local requirements.

2. **Protected Classes:** Ensure this eviction does not violate fair housing laws or constitute retaliation against a tenant for exercising legal rights.

3. **Rent Control:** If the property is subject to rent control or just cause eviction ordinances, additional restrictions may apply.

4. **Self-Help Eviction Prohibited:** Do NOT attempt to forcibly remove the tenant, change locks, shut off utilities, or remove tenant's belongings. This is illegal and may result in significant liability.

5. **Legal Review Recommended:** Consult with a qualified attorney before proceeding with eviction to ensure compliance with all applicable laws.
