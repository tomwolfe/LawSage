- [Other routine matters]

**Supermajority Vote Required** ([Percentage]% ownership) for:
- Admitting new Partners
- Approving capital expenditures over $[Amount]
- Entering contracts valued over $[Amount]
- [Other significant matters]

**Unanimous Consent Required** for:
- Amending this Agreement
- Dissolving the Partnership
- Admitting new Partners
- Selling substantially all Partnership assets
- Incurring debt over $[Amount]
- [Other critical matters]

## ARTICLE VIII - DISSOLUTION AND LIQUIDATION

### 8.1 Events Causing Dissolution

The Partnership shall dissolve upon:

a) Unanimous agreement of Partners
b) Expiration of the Partnership term
c) Withdrawal, death, or bankruptcy of a Partner (unless Partnership continues)
d) Court order
e) [Other events]

### 8.2 Liquidation Process

Upon dissolution:

1. Partnership affairs shall be wound up
2. Assets shall be liquidated
3. Proceeds shall be distributed in the following order:
   a) Payment of Partnership debts and liabilities
   b) Return of capital contributions
   c) Distribution of remaining assets according to profit-sharing ratios

### 8.3 Certificate of Dissolution

Partners shall execute and file all necessary documents to dissolve the Partnership.
