# CHILD CUSTODY AGREEMENT

This Child Custody Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [Date] by and between:

**PARENT 1:** [Full Name], residing at [Address] ("Parent 1")

**PARENT 2:** [Full Name], residing at [Address] ("Parent 2")

(Collectively referred to as the "Parents")

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parents are the parents of the following minor child(ren):

- [Child's Full Name], born [Date of Birth] ("Child 1")
- [Child's Full Name], born [Date of Birth] ("Child 2")
- [Child's Full Name], born [Date of Birth] ("Child 3")

WHEREAS, the Parents desire to establish a custody and visitation arrangement that serves the best interests of the child(ren);

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the Parents agree as follows:

## ARTICLE I - LEGAL CUSTODY

### 1.1 Type of Legal Custody

☐ **Joint Legal Custody:** Both Parents shall share the right and responsibility to make decisions relating to the health, education, and welfare of the child(ren).

☐ **Sole Legal Custody:** Parent [1/2] shall have the exclusive right and responsibility to make decisions relating to the health, education, and welfare of the child(ren).

### 1.2 Decision-Making Areas

The Parents shall consult and agree on the following major decisions:

a) **Education:** School selection, tutoring, special education services, and extracurricular activities

b) **Healthcare:** Medical, dental, vision, and mental health treatment

c) **Religious Upbringing:** Religious affiliation and activities

d) **Extracurricular Activities:** Sports, arts, and other enrichment activities

### 1.3 Dispute Resolution

If the Parents cannot agree on a major decision, they shall:

a) First attempt mediation with a qualified family mediator
b) If mediation fails, submit the dispute to binding arbitration
c) As a last resort, petition the court for resolution

## ARTICLE II - PHYSICAL CUSTODY AND VISITATION

### 2.1 Primary Physical Custody

☐ **Joint Physical Custody:** The child(ren) shall spend approximately equal time with each Parent.

☐ **Primary Custody with Parent 1:** Parent 1 shall have primary physical custody, with visitation to Parent 2 as specified below.

☐ **Primary Custody with Parent 2:** Parent 2 shall have primary physical custody, with visitation to Parent 1 as specified below.

### 2.2 Regular Visitation Schedule

Each Parent shall be entitled to [Number] weeks of uninterrupted vacation time with the child(ren) during the summer school break, with [Number] weeks' notice required.

### 2.5 School Breaks

- **Winter Break:** The child(ren) shall spend the first half with one Parent and the second half with the other Parent, alternating each year.
- **Spring Break:** The child(ren) shall alternate between Parents each year.

## ARTICLE III - TRANSPORTATION AND EXCHANGES

### 3.1 Exchange Location

Exchanges shall occur at:
☐ The child(ren)'s school
☐ [Neutral public location]
☐ The residence of the Parent having custody

### 3.2 Transportation Responsibility

☐ The Parent receiving the child(ren) shall provide transportation
☐ Parent 1 shall provide all transportation
☐ Parent 2 shall provide all transportation
☐ Transportation shall be shared equally

### 3.3 Late Policy

If a Parent is delayed more than [Number] minutes, they must notify the other Parent immediately. Repeated violations may result in modification of this Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV - CHILD SUPPORT

### 4.1 Support Amount

Parent [1/2] shall pay child support in the amount of $[Amount] per month, payable on the [Day] of each month.

### 4.2 Payment Method

Payments shall be made through:
☐ Wage garnishment
☐ Direct deposit
☐ Check
☐ State child support services

### 4.3 Additional Expenses

The Parents shall share the following expenses [proportionally/as specified]:

a) **Medical Expenses:** Unreimbursed medical, dental, and vision expenses
b) **Childcare:** Daycare, after-school care, and babysitting
c) **Extracurricular Activities:** Reasonable costs for activities
d) **Educational Expenses:** Tuition, supplies, and tutoring

## ARTICLE V - HEALTH INSURANCE AND MEDICAL CARE

### 5.1 Health Insurance

Parent [1/2] shall maintain health insurance coverage for the child(ren) through their employer or private insurance.

### 5.2 Medical Decision-Making

The Parent with legal custody shall make routine medical decisions. Emergency medical care may be authorized by either Parent.

### 5.3 Medical Information

Both Parents shall have access to all medical records and the right to communicate with healthcare providers.

## ARTICLE VI - EDUCATION

### 6.1 School Selection

The child(ren) shall attend [School Name] unless both Parents agree to a change.

### 6.2 School Communication

Both Parents shall:
- Receive copies of report cards and school communications
- Be listed as emergency contacts
- Have the right to attend school events and conferences

## ARTICLE VII - COMMUNICATION

### 7.1 Parent-Child Communication

Each Parent shall facilitate reasonable telephone/video communication between the child(ren) and the other Parent during their parenting time.

### 7.2 Parent-Parent Communication

The Parents shall communicate regarding the child(ren) through:
☐ Phone calls
☐ Text messages
☐ Email
☐ Co-parenting application (e.g., OurFamilyWizard, TalkingParents)

## ARTICLE VIII - RELOCATION

### 8.1 Notice Requirement

Either Parent intending to relocate more than [Number] miles from their current residence must provide [Number] days' written notice to the other Parent.

### 8.2 Modification

If relocation significantly impacts the custody arrangement, the Parents shall negotiate a modification or seek court intervention.

## ARTICLE IX - MODIFICATION

This Agreement may be modified only by written agreement signed by both Parents or by court order.

## ARTICLE X - GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [State].

## ARTICLE XI - ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parents and supersedes all prior agreements regarding custody and visitation.
